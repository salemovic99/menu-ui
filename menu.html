The Secret Sauce

In 1930, Chef Boubier invented a sauce so singular it outlived everything else he ever created — named after his restaurant on Rue Mont-Blanc 26, Geneva. Four generations later, one man holds the secret: Mr. François Vouillamoz, who prepares it in his laboratory beneath the original restaurant and ships it by air to every table. Inherited by one person per generation. Never sold. Never replicated.

The Ritual of the Table

Your cut arrives raw and rested. The sauce is poured at your table, over a living flame — a tradition unchanged since 1930.
